6.3 DEATH BY MEDICINE
The practice of medicine causes more deaths in US than any chronic disease even heart disease. There are over 700,000 deaths caused due to medical care. This includes failed surgeries, adverse drug reactions, dug side effects etc.. 
This is well articulated in a documentary “ Death by Medicine” by Garry Null.
